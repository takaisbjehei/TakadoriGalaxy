Chapter One

Our Love Story

Every great romance begins with a single moment — a glance, a word, a heartbeat that refuses to settle. This is ours, told one chapter at a time.

The First Glance
where it began

The day the world paused

Across a crowded room, a glance held a second too long. The world dimmed around her, and somewhere inside him, a door he didn't know he had quietly opened.

The First Word
hello, you

A simple hello, a forever beginning

She laughed at something silly he said. He memorized the sound. From that breath onward, every conversation felt like coming home to a place he hadn't yet seen.

The First Date
under the lights

Two hearts, one quiet evening

Stars hung low. Coffee turned cold. Time slipped through their fingers like silk, and neither of them tried to catch it — because for once, slowness was the most precious thing in the world.

The First "I Love You"
the brave words

The bravest three words ever spoken

It slipped out before he could rehearse it — and her smile, slow and sure, told him she had been waiting all along. The universe held its breath. Then it sighed in relief.

A Thousand Tomorrows
still us

And every day after that

The chapters keep writing themselves. Quiet mornings. Rainy afternoons. Inside jokes only the two of them will ever understand. The story isn't finished — it's only just begun.
