Moonlit Soul

In the silence of the night,

Your name is my only song.

A moonbeam's gentle light,

Where our souls both belong.


You are the dream I never

knew I was dreaming,

The star that found me lost,

and gave my sky its meaning.

The warmth within the dark,

the compass guiding home.

A Letter From My Heart

?
Written under the same stars that brought us together
My Dearest Aleena,

I find myself writing this because words are the only vessels large enough to carry the weight of what I feel for you. In a world of fleeting moments, you are my absolute — my constant, my gravity, my home.

Every time I see you, it's like seeing the first light of dawn after the longest winter. You have this way of making the ordinary feel extraordinary — a simple conversation becomes poetry, a stolen glance becomes a memory I'll carry forever.

You've taught me that love isn't just a feeling, but a place — a home I never knew existed until I held your hand. You are the reason I believe in magic, in destiny, in the idea that two souls can be written for each other across lifetimes.

Thank you for choosing me, Aleena. Thank you for loving the unpolished parts of my soul, for being the poetry in my life's prose, and for making every heartbeat feel like a love song. I promise to cherish every sunrise and every storm with you — forever and beyond.

Forever yours, in this life and every life after,
Alfaiz ♥
